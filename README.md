
# The Encounters

An Educational Game developed by 3 Student Game Developers at Temasek Polytechnic, to raise awareness of the common types of Mental Health by giving the player quests to complete and learn more about the characters with mental issues.

## FAQ

#### 1. What type of platform can TheEncounters be played on?

Currently supporting Windows Desktop.

#### 2. Does TheEncounters has multiplayer mode?

Single-player only.

#### 3. Is TheEncounters based on real life situations?

Partially Yes, as we are working with Singapore Association for Mental Health (SAMH). However, due to potential PDPA violations, we do not planned to develop in complete mirror of the past experiences that patient with mental issue has.

#### 4. How can TheEncounters raise awareness of Mental Health?

Player is roleplayed as a volunteer at a fictional school and 4 new students with different mental issue will be introduced in the early game. The player has to find out what mental issue each student has through a quest-based system and 5 days.
